Miserere

A short film under development. Scroll down to find more information on the project.

Synopsis

After breaking off the affair with her masked lover, Beth, a married woman and a mother, must fight to keep her multiple lives and identities apart. The scorned love is indeed a forceful one.

Details

  • Runtime (estimated): 20-30min
  • Film/Production Language: English
  • Location: Berlin
  • Shooting date: Q1 2017

Crew

Characters

Beth

Protagonist. A young mother in her late twenties. She is beautiful and elegant but marred by sadness. There is a fickle nervousness to her, as if she might take off at any moment. May come across as distant and cold but at the same time, she is very caring and loving towards her child.

The role requires the actress to be very active in developing the lines and character.

Requirements

  • 20-40 years old female (screen age: 27-30)
  • English-speaker (not necessarily native tongue)

If you're interested in the role, please send CV, headshots and examples of work or any other relevant details to erasmusdearos+miserere@gmail.com.

Adam

Adam is the masked lover. He is in his late thirties and has a rough, passionate but tender manner. Impatient with an addictive personality. Direct in his speech and questioning. This character is masked throughout the film and face never revealed, so actor must be comfortable in acting with only voice and body language.

The role requires the actor to be very active in developing the lines and character.

Requirements

  • 25-45 years old male (screen age: 35-40)
  • English-speaker (not necessarily native tongue)
  • Strong body & vocal actor
  • Comfortable with nudity

If you're interested in the role, please send CV, headshots and examples of work or any other relevant details to erasmusdearos+miserere@gmail.com..

Husband

Beth's husband is a man in his fourties. Clean and handsome, but boringly so. Speaks with authority and certainty. Possibly a politician or well-off business-man. Voice is very important, as character will not feature a lot on screen.

Requirements

  • 35-60 years old male (screen age: 45-50)
  • English-speaker (not necessarily native tongue)
  • Strong vocal actor

If you're interested in the role, please send CV, headshots and examples of work or any other relevant details to erasmusdearos+miserere@gmail.com..

Child

Beth's 7-year-old son. Happy and easily excitable. Somewhat confused by his mother's somewhat erratic behaviour.

Requirements

  • 6-12 years old male (screen age: 7)
  • Should be comfortable with cameras and acting
  • English-speaking

If you're interested in the role, please send CV, headshots and examples of work or any other relevant details to erasmusdearos+miserere@gmail.com..

Locations

  1. INT. Ultra-violet flat - night: The lover's space. Exotic and otherworldly. Lit by a surreal UV-light.
  2. INT. Staircase - night: Rundown staircase leading to UV flat.
  3. EXT. Building complex - night: Establishing shot
  4. INT. Clean, well-lit flat - day/evening: Husband & Beth's flat. Expensive and lit in bright white light.
  5. EXT. Park / Playgrund - day/night: Joyful recreational area with bench and rubbish bin.
