Miserere

Miserere (working title) is a short film under development. Find more information about the project below.

Synopsis

After breaking off the affair with her masked lover, Beth, a married woman and a mother, must fight to keep her two lives apart. The scorned love is indeed an uncontrollable one.

Details

  • Runtime (estimated): 10-20min
  • Film/Production Language: English
  • Location: Berlin
  • Shooting date (estimated): August, 2016

Characters

Beth

Protagonist. A young mother in her mid-twenties. She is beautiful and elegant but marred by sadness. She is both hard and soft. There is a fickle nervousness to her, as if she might take off at any moment. May come across as distant and cold but at the same time, she is very caring and loving towards her child.

Requirements

  • 20-35 years old female
  • English-speaker (not necessarily native tongue)

Adam

Adam is the masked lover. He is in his thirties and has a rough, passionate but tender manner. Impatient with an addictive personality. Direct in his speech and questioning. This character is masked throughout the film and face never revealed, so actor must be comfortable in acting with only voice and body language.
