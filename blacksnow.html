Black snow

On the brink of closing the biggest deal in company history, Adam is finally about to separate himself from the bottom feeders and ascend into the higher echelons of the city's hierarchy. But when the venture is suddenly obstructed by Mr Hart & Mr Loveless, para-legal counsellors par excellence, Adam is forced into a fight for everything that he's spent his life building. Much can be created in a week, much destroyed. Seven days is all Adam has.
